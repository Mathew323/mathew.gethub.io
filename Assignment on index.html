His Excellency,

President Bola Ahmad Tinubu G.C.F.R.,

Presidential Villa,

Aso Rock,

Abuja, FCT.

Dear Mr President,

LETTER OF INCONVINENCY OF THE CURRENT SITUATION OF THE ECONOMY IN NIGERIA

I hereby write to Your Excellency to express my feelings as regards to the above topic.

Sir, since the day of handing over to you, you started on a bad side thinking you will get it right along the way but till you're strugling with the whole system. Although, right from campaign period we know something is wrong somewhere but those pushing you to do more are nowhere to be found now, that's life bro. By now, all Nigerians know that Pharaoh treated the Isrealites well back then comparing to what's happening now in this country.

Actually, when the Bible said "...the heart of men is desperately wicked..." I thougth there's an exemption there but you prove me wrong, wickedness starts from you bro. Hope you're aware that those supporting back then are all suffering apart from those you put in power with you even more than we the innocent.

Please, it's never too late to amend your ways. Because the next action of the good citizens will definitely shock you bro. And if it's getting out of hands, you can as well resign and let's someone up to task take over instead of you making people to suffer aimlessly.

I will like to drop my pen at this point, in case you want to come for me, just come alone and don't set me up with hidden men, let's do it one-on-one bro. It's high time you get it right bro.

Your Concerned Citizen

Matthew

5th September, 2024.
