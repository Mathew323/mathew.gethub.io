Our Services at Ihotu Coding Academy

Empowering you with practical skills and industry knowledge to launch your tech career.

Web Development

Our coding academy offers a comprehensive web development program covering front-end and back-end technologies. Learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular, along with server-side skills in Node.js, Express, and database management.

Data Science and Machine Learning

Equip yourself with skills in Python, R, data analysis, and machine learning. Our academy provides hands-on training with libraries like TensorFlow and PyTorch, preparing students for roles in data analysis, data science, and machine learning engineering.

Mobile App Development

We offer specialized programs for mobile development in both iOS and Android. Master Swift for iOS or Kotlin for Android, and create your own apps through project-based learning and real-world applications.

Cybersecurity

Our cybersecurity courses provide essential knowledge on network security, ethical hacking, and compliance. Gain the skills needed to protect systems from threats, conduct vulnerability assessments, and ensure data integrity.

DevOps and Cloud Computing

Learn how to work with popular cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure. Our DevOps program includes training on CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, and other skills to automate and manage large-scale applications effectively.

Career Support

We provide career support services, including resume workshops, mock interviews, and networking opportunities. Our partnerships with tech companies and hiring partners open doors for students to find job opportunities after graduation.
