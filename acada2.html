About Us

Ihotu Coding Academy is an educational institution focused on empowering students with essential technology skills, particularly in software development and programming. Founded with the goal of bridging gaps in technology education, Ihotu Coding Academy offers a range of coding courses designed to cater to diverse learning needs and skill levels, from beginners to advanced learners. The academy emphasizes hands-on learning, encouraging students to work on real-world projects to reinforce their coding skills.

Key aspects of Ihotu Coding Academy’s curriculum include languages and tools widely used in the tech industry, such as Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Additionally, students are introduced to broader concepts like web development, app development, and data science, equipping them with a well-rounded foundation in technology. Beyond technical skills, Ihotu Coding Academy also promotes problem-solving, critical thinking, and collaboration, preparing students for the dynamics of the modern workforce. With mentorship from industry professionals, Ihotu Coding Academy provides networking opportunities and career guidance, making it a valuable resource for anyone looking to enter or advance in the tech field.
