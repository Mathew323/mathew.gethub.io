CEO, Ihotu Coding Academy

Matthew is the visionary CEO of Ihotu Coding Academy, a leading institution committed to empowering students with essential coding skills and fostering tech-driven innovation. With a background in both education and technical expertise, Matthew has dedicated his career to transforming the learning landscape through technology and inclusive education.
